Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27-06-2023

Welcome to Mann Sign!

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you provide when using our online poster ordering app. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we collect the following personal information for your user profile:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Login information

2. Storage and Security

We store the collected information on our secure servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

We use encryption for login information to enhance the security of your data.

3. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. Your information is strictly used for the purpose of providing you with our poster ordering services.

4. Cookies

We currently do not use cookies or similar technologies on our Android app. However, please note that third-party services or partners we work with may use cookies or similar technologies. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies for more information.

5. Access and Update of Personal Information

You have the right to access and update the personal information in your user profile. You can make changes to your profile details and reset your password through OTP authentication.

6. Account Deletion

If you have no pending orders, you have the option to delete your account. Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have any pending orders, please contact our support team for assistance.

7. Non-Personal Information

We do not collect any additional non-personal information or usage data on our app.

8. Age Restrictions

There are no specific age restrictions for using our app. However, we recommend that individuals under the age of 18 seek parental consent before using our services.

9. Notification of Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by sending an email to the address provided in your user profile. It is your responsibility to review the updated policy.
